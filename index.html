DJKT

Listen Contact

Music, production, and a little voltage.

Originally emerging from Chicago's vibrant nightlife scene and now based in Los Angeles, DJKT is a dynamic DJ, producer, and lifelong musician known for creating unforgettable dance floor experiences. DJKT's signature sound blends deep house, classic Chicago house, and tropical house, creating soulful, groove-driven sets that balance uplifting energy with infectious rhythms.

Over the years, DJKT has built an impressive résumé of headlining events, residencies, and event appearances across Chicago and Los Angeles and beyond. Throughout their career, DJKT has shared stages and lineups with acclaimed artists including Terri Bristol, Psycho Bitch, Curt Cameruci (Flosstradamus/Autobot), Ruby Rose, JD Samson, and Toy Connor, further cementing her reputation as a respected fixture in the dance music and nightlife communities.

Outside the DJ booth, DJKT is an accomplished drummer, songwriter, and producer who has performed at legendary venues including The Metro, Subterranean, Bottom Lounge, Double Door, and Whiskey A Go-Go. Whether commanding a packed nightclub, festival stage, or private event, DJKT delivers a powerful blend of musicality, energy, and connection that keeps audiences coming back for more.
